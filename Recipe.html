chinese noodles

Ingridents required

  • raw noodles
  • chinese noodles masala
  • onion
  • salt
  • vegetables
  • soya saus and tomato saus

Steps to be followed:

  1. Boil the noodles until cooked to soft in one utensil
  2. heat the oil and add mustard seeds choped onion in another utensil
  3. Add chinese masala as per taste and soya and tomato saus and add some water and boil for 3 mins
  4. add the boiled noodles in the masala ready boil it for 4 mins more and serve it hot.
